About Us.

Rentomojo is a furniture company. We enable the urban Indian to live better today in their homes, by giving them award-winning furniture and home decor on rent. All of our furniture is designed in-house by experts. Our furniture is inspired by the Indian urban user and how they live in their homes. Our designs are user-led and inspired by their evolving needs and growing lifestyle. So, with a simple click, one can furnish their entire home, room or corner without traversing multiple stores or websites at a fraction of the time, effort and cost. All decor that we provide is brand new, and never reused for hygiene reasons.
